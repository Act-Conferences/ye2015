Breaks and meals

Thanks to our sponsors' support, a pre-conference dinner, the coffee-breaks, as well as lunches and the conference dinner will be available for all the attendees to YAPC::EU 2015.

A pre-conference dinner will be celebrated on Tuesday, 1 of September. Our sponsor Eligo will invite us to pizza and drinks, from 19:00 to 21:00, at the Conference venue's inner garden. You can get more information if you see the venue's map.

Coffee-breaks are scheduled for Wednesday, Thursday and Friday at 10:30 and 16:30, lasting no more than 30 minuntes. They will be served next to the rooms where the talks will take place.

Lunches will start every day at 13:00, lasting 90 minutes, and are sponsored by Booking.

Lunches are organized as a tapas crawl, since Granada is well-known for an ancient tradition of complementing your drink (in bars and cafes) with a small amount of food (the tapa). We have reached an agreement with many bars sorrounding the Conference venue that will accept Coinions (the "official" Conference's coin) as the way to pay for your drink and it's associated tapa.

Thus, when registering yourself at the Conference's registration desk, you will get 20 Coinions, divided into 10 tickets. So, feel free to spend them as you prefer, probably visiting the different bars associated with this initiative.

In short, we'll provide a list of the bars where Coinions are accepted, including their locations.

Finally, the Conference Dinner will take place on Wednesday (2 of September), 21:00 hours, at Hotel Los Angeles. This dinner is included in your registration, but you can buy addtional tickets for your guests.
